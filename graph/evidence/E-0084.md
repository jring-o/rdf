# E-0084 — Portico pricing

Portico institutional membership ranges **$1,500-$25,462 per year**, scaled to library budget. The consortium reports **1,076 participating publishers and 1,288 supporting libraries** in its *2023 Year in Review*.

For C-0030, Portico's pricing reinforces the surviving-consortium pattern: proportional, scale-aware fees with a low entry point ($1,500 floor) that admits small-library participation while still aggregating enough total revenue to sustain operations across publisher and library customer bases. The combined membership volume — over 2,300 organizational participants — is the economic base that the continuous-operation pattern in C-0030 depends on.
