# E-0064 — Federal AI investment scale 2024-2026

Federal AI investment in 2026 spans multiple agencies and exceeds **$3.3 billion per year in nondefense research** alone, plus several billion more in defense applications.

- **NSF NAIRR Pilot** (S-0119, S-0120): launched January 2024 with 10 federal agency partners + 25 nongovernmental partners; by 2026 expanded to 14 + 28.
- **NSF National AI Research Institutes** (S-0123): 29 institutes funded at ~$20M each over 5 years.
- **DOE Office of Science AI + Genesis Mission** (S-0124): EO November 24, 2025; backed by $320M+ DOE investments announced December 2025 across American Science Cloud, Transformational AI Models Consortium, automated-laboratory projects.
- **DARPA AI Next + AI Forward** (S-0126): $2B+ DARPA AI investment since 2018.
- **DOD Chief Digital and AI Office** (S-0125).

Aggregate (S-0127): ~$3.3B/yr nondefense AI R&D plus several billion more in defense applications. EDUCAUSE *Top 10 IT Issues* 2025 ranks AI and data as the top institutional priority (S-0118).

The case establishes the magnitude of the federal funding stream institutions are competing for, and the specificity of the data-management capacity, reproducibility, and access governance criteria the major solicitations now embed. Institutions without credible AI-data infrastructure are outside the competitive frontier for the largest federal research opportunity of the decade.
