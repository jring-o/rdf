# E-0092 — OSTP Nelson Memo

The Office of Science and Technology Policy memorandum "Ensuring Free, Immediate, and Equitable Access to Federally Funded Research" (the Nelson Memo, August 25, 2022) directs **all federal agencies to update public-access policies eliminating the 12-month embargo for publications and data**. Agency plans were to be finalized by December 31, 2024 and in effect by end of 2025. The memo covers **more than $90 billion in annual federal research funding**.

For C-0003 and C-0025: the Nelson Memo is the cross-agency umbrella under which the NIH DMS Policy (E-0044), NSF DMP gating (E-0091), and successor-agency public-access requirements operate. Its operational consequence is that immediate open access to data — not just publications — becomes the default federal funding condition across the research enterprise. The architectural question of whether a grantee can in fact produce the data on inspection becomes, under the Nelson Memo's coverage, a question every federally funded researcher in the United States must answer.
