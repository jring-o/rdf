# E-0102 — EuroHPC Federation Platform and AI Factories

The European High Performance Computing Joint Undertaking's **EuroHPC Federation Platform** development began January 2025 under CSC–IT Center for Science leadership, with **first release on April 15, 2026** providing a unified single access point to EuroHPC JU systems with federated authentication and authorization. The companion **AI Factories program** operates around EuroHPC supercomputing facilities to support European AI ecosystem development, with **19 AI Factories and 13 Antennas operational across the EU as of late 2025**.

For C-0039: EuroHPC's combination of federated platform access and AI Factories network is a continent-scale demonstration of what coordinated AI/data infrastructure looks like when the governance and funding model is aligned with the technical architecture. The AI Factories provide computational and data-governance capabilities that competing U.S. R1 institutions, operating in fragmented Tier 0 / Tier 1 configurations, cannot match without coordinated federation.
