# S-0129 — AI faculty market

The AI faculty market is the most competitive in academia, driven by a 2-3x compensation differential between academic and industry AI roles, creating sustained retention pressure on institutions. Offer-acceptance rates depend on institutional data and compute infrastructure that determines whether candidates can run the research their industry counterparts could fund directly.
