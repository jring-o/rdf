# S-0086 — Physical plant + cabling

Higher-ed physical-plant amortizes over 10-15-year cycles for mechanical and electrical infrastructure, longer for building shells. Cabling accounts for 61% of campus networking infrastructure costs and is embedded in buildings regardless of traffic.
