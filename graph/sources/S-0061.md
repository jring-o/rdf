# S-0061 — HERD FY2023 + Carnegie R1

NCSES *Higher Education Research and Development Survey: FY2023*, NSF, 2024. 2025 Carnegie classification (R1: Very High Spending and Doctorate Production, $50M+ R&D and 70+ doctorates, FY2021-FY2023 HERD data).

**Key facts:** Total US academic R&D ~$108.8B across 914 reporting institutions; top 30 institutions account for 42% of total. 187 R1 institutions designated in 2025.
