# S-0014 — CLOCKSS

CLOCKSS Archive. Preservation statistics and *Triggered Content* register, clockss.org and clockss.org/triggered-content/, accessed April 2026.

**Key facts:** 63.6 million articles. 568,210 books. 12 mirror nodes. 300 libraries. 691 publishers. 81 triggered titles released to the public on closure of the original publisher.

**Use:** Tier 2 exemplar for journal preservation; ~20-year operational history; demonstrates the trigger model under which preservation copies become public on publisher failure.
