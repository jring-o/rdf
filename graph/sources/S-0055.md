# S-0055 — Piwowar & Vision 2013

Piwowar, H.A. & Vision, T.J. "Data reuse and the open data citation advantage." *PeerJ* 1:e175, 2013. doi:10.7717/peerj.175.

**Key facts:** 9% citation advantage for papers with publicly available data, controlled for journal, author, and institutional history. ~150 reuse papers per 100 deposited datasets within 5 years.
